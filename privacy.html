Privacy Policy

WYD: Daily Journal

This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices you have.

Effective Date

May 10, 2026

WYD ("we", "us", or "our") provides a journaling and habit-tracking mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: email address, display name, and profile data collected when you create an account.
  • User content: journal entries, weekly reports, and photos or media you attach to entries.
  • Usage and analytics: app usage events, feature usage, and crash reports.
  • Device information: device model, OS version, locale, and unique identifiers necessary for service operation.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and operate the app, including storing and presenting your journals and reports.
  • To communicate account-related information and support.
  • To analyze app usage and improve features and stability.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing and Third Parties

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We share data with service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as hosting and database providers. We use Supabase for backend storage and authentication.
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights or safety.

4. Data Retention & Security

  • We retain data as long as your account exists or as necessary to provide the service.
  • We protect data using industry-standard security practices, but cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights & Choices

  • You can request access, correction, or deletion of your account and content by contacting us.
  • You can disable analytics in-app if we provide that option.
