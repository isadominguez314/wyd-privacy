Privacy Policy for WYD: Daily Journal

Effective date: May 10, 2026 WYD ("we", "us", or "our") provides a journaling and habit-tracking mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

- Account information: email address, display name, profile data (collected when you create an account). - User content: journal entries, weekly reports, photos or media you attach to entries. - Usage and analytics: app usage events, feature usage, and crash reports. - Device information: device model, OS version, locale, and unique identifiers necessary for service operation.

2. How We Use Information

- To provide and operate the app (store and present your journals and reports). - To communicate account-related information and support. - To analyze app usage and improve features and stability. - To comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing and Third Parties

- We do not sell your personal data. - We share data with service providers who perform services on our behalf (for example, hosting and database providers). We use Supabase for backend storage and authentication. - We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights or safety.

4. Data Retention & Security

- We retain data as long as your account exists or as necessary to provide the service. - We protect data using industry-standard security practices, but cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights & Choices

- You can request access, correction, or deletion of your account and content by contacting us (see below). - You can disable analytics in-app if we provide that option.
